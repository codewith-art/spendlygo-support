FAQs

How do I restore purchases?

Open the app → Settings → Restore Purchases. Make sure you’re signed in with the same Apple ID used for purchase.

How do I upgrade to SpendlyGo Premium?

Open the app → Settings → Premium to view plans and subscribe via Apple.

Where is my data stored?

By default, data stays on your device. You can enable iCloud sync or connect to your selected ERP destination if configured.

How do I export or delete my data?

You can export receipts from the Report page or individual Receipt pages. To send data to an accounting ERP, go to Settings → Share Destinations and add your endpoint. You can also export or delete data under Settings → Data Tools.

Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2025-10-08

Overview

SpendlyGo helps you capture and organize receipts. We aim to process data on device whenever possible and minimize data sharing.

Data You Provide

  • Receipt images and text you scan
  • Receipt details you edit (merchant, total, tax, date, category)
  • Optional notes and categories

How We Process Data

By default, extraction runs on device. You may enable cloud processing to improve recognition. When enabled, relevant receipt text and images may be sent securely to our processing service for extraction, then returned to your device.

Storage & Sync

Receipt data and images are stored locally on your device. If you enable iCloud, data may sync across your devices via Apple. In Business mode, data may sync with your company systems per your workspace configuration.

Purchases

Subscriptions and restores are handled by Apple. We may use RevenueCat to securely manage entitlements and localized pricing; RevenueCat receives purchase metadata needed to validate your subscription. We do not receive your full payment details.

Analytics & Tracking

We do not use third‑party advertising trackers. Any diagnostics are used to improve stability and features.

Your Choices

  • On‑device only processing toggle
  • Enable/disable iCloud sync
  • Export or delete your data using the app’s data tools

Security

We use industry‑standard measures to protect data in transit and at rest. No method is 100% secure.

Children

The app is not directed to children under the age required by local law.

Changes

We may update this policy; material changes will be reflected here. Continued use constitutes acceptance.
